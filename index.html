Hair Concerns

What is your hair asking for?

Recognise your concern. Find the right approach. Build the right routine.

Excessive Shedding

More hair than usual on your pillow, in your brush or blocking the shower drain.

Weak or Thinning Edges

Fragile hairline, sparse temples or a receding edge that affects your confidence.

Dry, Itchy Scalp

Persistent dryness, flakiness or discomfort that won't go away no matter what you try.

Stubborn Dandruff

White or yellowish flakes that are embarrassing, persistent and hard to manage.

Hair Breakage

Hair that snaps mid-strand, appears uneven or thins visibly despite regular care.

Poor Moisture Retention

Hair that feels dry hours after washing and refuses to hold moisture throughout the week.

Damaged Hair

Dull, rough or brittle hair damaged by heat, colour, tension or years of neglect.

Slow Hair Progress

Hair that refuses to grow, or grows but constantly breaks off before retaining any length.

BANGLOG HAIR MAGIC products are cosmetic haircare products, not medical treatments. Customers with severe or sudden hair or scalp concerns should consult a qualified healthcare professional.
